# GRID Playbooks

Version: Playbook System v1.0

## Purpose

GRID Playbooks convert repeatable experience into reusable institutional knowledge.

Every repeatable success becomes a playbook. Every major failure becomes a lesson. Every validated process becomes a reusable operating asset.

## Doctrine

THE GRID does not merely remember outcomes. It converts validated experience into reusable playbooks.

## Status Rules

- Draft: proposed or placeholder process; not validated.
- Tested: used at least once with evidence, but not yet repeatable.
- Validated: repeated with evidence and approved by Mission Commander.
- Retired: preserved for history but no longer recommended.

## Playbook Types

- Revenue Playbooks.
- Trading Playbooks.
- Engineering Playbooks.
- Operations Playbooks.
- Knowledge Playbooks.
- Customer Playbooks.
- Launch Playbooks.

## Promotion Rule

A playbook may not become Validated until it has:

- Repeated real-world use.
- Related Mission Records.
- Related Evidence Ledger entries.
- Clear success and failure criteria.
- Lessons learned from actual outcomes.
- Mission Commander review.

## Boundaries

- No fake wins.
- No fake lessons.
- No fake customer results.
- No fake trading edge.
- No completed playbooks unless evidence exists.
- Draft placeholders must remain clearly marked Draft.
