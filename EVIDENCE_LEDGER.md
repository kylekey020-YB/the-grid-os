# Evidence Ledger

Version: Launch Intelligence v1.0

## Purpose

Maintain a chronological record of verified market and execution evidence for THE GRID.

This ledger records what is known, when it was observed, where the evidence came from, and which mission or launch it supports.

## Doctrine

- Track only real evidence.
- Do not infer market response from launch status.
- Unknown values remain Unknown or Awaiting Evidence.
- Execution metrics are controlled by THE GRID.
- Market metrics are controlled by customers, platforms, and external demand.
- Revenue, reviews, orders, repeat customers, and validated trading edges require explicit evidence.

## Playbook Evidence

Playbooks may reference Evidence Ledger entries, but a playbook is not validated merely because it exists. Validation requires repeated evidence, Mission Records, lessons learned, and Mission Commander review.

## Evidence Standard

Every future entry should include:

- Date.
- Evidence type.
- Related Mission ID.
- Related launch, product, backtest, or customer record.
- Evidence source.
- Whether the evidence is verified, awaiting confirmation, or rejected.

No screenshots, account exports, customer data, secrets, API keys, broker data, wallet data, or private platform records should be committed unless sanitized and approved.

## Alpha Evidence

Alpha Lab records may reference Evidence Ledger entries, but an alpha is not validated because it exists in the Alpha Library.

Alpha promotion requires real data provenance, backtest evidence, out-of-sample validation, baseline comparison, risk review, and Mission Commander approval.

Awaiting evidence:

- First validated alpha hypothesis.
- First completed WRAITH-LSTM real-data validation.
- First alpha rejected after evidence review.
- First alpha promoted to paper-test candidate.
