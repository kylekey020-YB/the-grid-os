## Board Rules

- Draft means an idea exists but evidence is not assembled.
- Research means evidence is being gathered.
- Scoring means evidence is being ranked or compared.
- Approved means Mission Commander approved the next bounded step.
- Building means implementation or production is underway.
- Launched means the work is externally visible or operational.
- Measuring means real results are being tracked.
- Playbook means the workflow is repeatable and documented.
- Archived means the mission is closed but preserved.

## Approval Boundary

Any move into Approved, Launched, automation, spending, customer messaging, trading risk, supplier outreach, or main branch merge requires Mission Commander approval.
