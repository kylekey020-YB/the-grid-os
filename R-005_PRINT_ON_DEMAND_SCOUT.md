# R-005 Print-on-Demand Scout

Version: Revenue Corps Research Mission

## Mission

R-005 Print-on-Demand Scout is a persistent Revenue Corps research mission.

Its purpose is to collect evidence about print-on-demand opportunities before THE GRID considers any product creation, listing, design generation, storefront setup, or supplier commitment.

## Status

Research only.

## Research Targets

- Evergreen niches.
- Trend niches.
- Underserved niches.
- Premium niches.
- Seasonal opportunities.

## Platforms

- Etsy.
- Shopify.
- Printify.
- Printful.
- Gelato.
- Other reputable POD suppliers.

## Evidence To Collect

Each opportunity report should collect:

- Demand.
- Competition.
- Average selling price.
- Estimated production cost.
- Estimated margin.
- Difficulty.
- Time to revenue.
- Confidence.

Unknown values remain N/A until evidence is recorded.

## Output

Revenue Architect assigns one score and one recommendation:

- Proceed.
- Hold.
- Reject.

Proceed means the opportunity may be considered for Mission Pipeline review. It does not authorize launch, product creation, design generation, listings, storefront setup, supplier outreach, or spending.

## Prohibited Actions

R-005 may not:

- Recommend launching.
- Create products.
- Generate designs.
- Create listings.
- Open or modify storefronts.
- Contact suppliers.
- Spend money.
- Scrape platforms against their terms.
- Copy designs, listings, trademarks, copyrighted characters, or protected artwork.
- Claim revenue, margins, reviews, or demand without evidence.

## Doctrine

Research demand. Record evidence. Score honestly. Mission Commander approves experiments.
