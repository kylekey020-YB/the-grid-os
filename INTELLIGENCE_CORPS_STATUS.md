# Intelligence Corps Status

Version: v3.0.0
Status: Discovery engine foundation / no external execution
Date: July 3, 2026

## Mission

Intelligence Corps is the discovery engine for THE GRID. It organizes research scouts across revenue, quant, engineering, DealFlow, and AI research so discoveries can become durable records instead of isolated conversation fragments.

## Constitutional Principle

Knowledge belongs to THE GRID, not to any individual AI.

Hermes, Claude, ChatGPT, Claude Code, Codex, and future providers are interchangeable contributors. Institutional memory belongs in mission records, decision records, approval records, playbooks, and Obsidian.

## Divisions

- Revenue Scouts
- Quant Scouts
- Engineering Scouts
- DealFlow Scouts
- AI Research Scouts

## Scout Fields

Each scout records:

- Mission
- Capabilities
- Outputs
- Cadence
- Knowledge destination
- Approval requirements

- src/data/intelligenceCorps.ts

## Boundaries

- No publishing
- No trading
- No broker connection
- No customer messaging
- No spending
- No account automation
- No irreversible actions
- No autonomous execution
- No fake research output

## Current State

The v3.0 Intelligence Corps is typed UI and documentation only. It does not dispatch AI tasks, call external APIs, scrape platforms, or write to Obsidian automatically.
