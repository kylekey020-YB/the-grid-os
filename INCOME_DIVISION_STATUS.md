# THE GRID Income Division Status

Current Version: v0.5.0

## Purpose

The Income Division is a planning foundation for future Etsy, Fiverr, affiliate, digital product, and creative automation workflows. It exists to study market patterns, design original products, validate demand, and publish manually before any automation is considered.

This document is planning only. It does not authorize scraping, autonomous posting, platform integrations, Telegram bots, or copied product creation.

## Current Goal

Reach the first $10k/month income milestone through validated, ethical, original workflows.

## Current Phase

Research and workflow design.

## Approved Lanes

1. Etsy digital products
2. Fiverr productized services
3. Affiliate / SEO content assets

## Planned Rooms

### Market Lab

Researches digital product opportunities, Etsy categories, Fiverr gigs, affiliate topics, and demand patterns.

### Design Forge

Creates original visual assets: thumbnails, Etsy designs, 2D game assets, POD designs, and templates.

### Copy Room

Drafts listings, Fiverr gigs, customer replies, emails, captions, and blog posts.

### Publishing Room

Tracks scheduled listings, posts, pins, uploads, and launch checklists.

### Revenue War Room

Tracks experiments, revenue, conversion, views, favorites, orders, and kill/scale decisions.

### Archives / Obsidian Vault

Stores every decision, product idea, prompt, result, failure, and winning pattern.

### Agent Profiles

Plans support for isolated specialist profiles:

- Designer
- Programmer
- Writer
- Researcher
- Operator
- Publisher

Each future profile should have:

- SOUL.md
- playbooks
- memory
- personality
- Telegram connection placeholder
- recurring jobs placeholder

## Prohibited

- Copying listings
- Trademark infringement
- Copyrighted characters
- Fake reviews
- Platform ToS violations
- Autonomous posting without review
- Scraping platforms without explicit review and compliance approval
- Live marketplace integrations before a validated manual workflow exists

## Doctrine

- Study patterns.
- Create original products.
- Validate demand.
- Publish manually first.
- Automate only repeated workflows.

## Current Safety State

Read-only planning. No live marketplace integrations, Telegram bots, scraping, autonomous posting, or platform actions are implemented.
