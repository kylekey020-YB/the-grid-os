# THE GRID Income Factory Status

Current Version: v0.7.0

## Purpose

The Income Factory is an assisted workflow foundation for the Income Division. It helps plan, review, and manually operate revenue experiments without autonomous publishing, platform scraping, account automation, auto-messaging, or spending money.

## Goal

First $10k/month through validated, ethical, original offers and manual publishing workflows.

## Current Phase

Assisted workflow foundation.

## Approved Lanes

- Etsy digital products
- Fiverr productized services
- Affiliate / SEO content assets
- YouTube thumbnails
- 2D game assets
- Templates / eBooks

## Prohibited

- Copying listings
- Copyrighted characters
- Trademarks
- Fake reviews
- Auto-publishing
- Auto-DMs
- Ad spend without approval
- Platform scraping that violates Terms
- Account automation
- Spending money without human approval

## Doctrine

- Research demand.
- Create original differentiated offers.
- Publish manually.
- Track results.
- Automate only proven repeated workflows.

## Manual Gates

Every publishable item must pass:

- Trademark check placeholder
- Copyright check placeholder
- Platform-ToS check placeholder
- Human approval required gate

## Current Safety State

Read-only UI/workflow foundation. No live marketplace integrations, autonomous publishing, auto-DMs, customer messaging, ad spend, account automation, or platform actions are implemented.
