# Opportunity Radar Status

Current Version: v2.6.0

## Status

Typed architecture / manual evidence only

## Mission

Every idea discovered by Revenue Corps enters Opportunity Radar.

Every opportunity gets documented, scored, and reviewed before entering the Mission Pipeline.

Opportunity hunting is a permanent function of THE GRID.

## Fields

- Name
- Marketplace
- Estimated Monthly Revenue
- Time to First Dollar
- Difficulty
- Evidence Level
- Competition
- Margin
- Recurring Revenue Potential
- Launch Cost
- Mission Fit
- Confidence
- Status
- Pipeline

## Pipeline

Idea -> Research -> Evidence -> Approval -> Launch -> Revenue -> Scale -> Automation

## Mission Commander Filters

- Highest ROI
- Fastest to launch
- Lowest cost
- Highest confidence
- Best recurring revenue

## Included Operating Areas

- Opportunity Radar
- Experiment Lab
- Revenue Forecasting
- Revenue Flywheel
- Product Foundry
- Marketplace Intelligence
- DealFlow Incubator
- Opportunity Queue

## Rules

- No backend.
- Typed architecture only.
- No fake data.
- Unknown values remain N/A.
- Forecasts are planning models only, not predictions or claims.
- Revenue Architect ranks opportunities by available evidence and explicit assumptions.
- Mission Commander approves experiments.
- No scraping integrations, account automation, customer messaging, autonomous publishing, spending, copied products, fake reviews, or fabricated scores.
