# Opportunity Radar

Backlinks: [[Revenue Architect]] [[Operation First Revenue]] [[Scout Reports]] [[Mission Pipeline]]

Opportunity Radar is the permanent Revenue Corps opportunity queue and ranking board.

Every opportunity is documented, scored, ranked, and reviewed before Mission Pipeline entry.

Current rules:

- Unknown values remain N/A.
- Forecasts are planning models only.
- Revenue Architect ranks opportunities.
- Mission Commander approves experiments.
- No backend, scraping integrations, account automation, autonomous publishing, spending, fake data, fabricated forecasts, or fabricated opportunity scores.
