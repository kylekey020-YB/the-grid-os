# Revenue Corps Status

Current Version: v2.3.0

## Status

Architecture Active / Research Only

## Mission

Revenue Corps is the official revenue operating department of THE GRID. Its mission is to turn Operation First Revenue into a disciplined portfolio of original, evidence-backed revenue streams.

Revenue Architect is no longer treated as a single researcher. Revenue Architect commands specialized teams that find opportunities, build original products after approval, and prepare manual launch packages.

## Command Structure

Revenue Architect

- Reports to Mission Commander.
- Ranks evidence.
- Selects strongest revenue paths for review.
- Recommends what enters the Mission Pipeline.
- Does not publish, spend, message customers, or automate accounts.

Revenue Intelligence

- Acts as the general staff.
- Scores scout reports when evidence exists.
- Compares opportunities.
- Maintains the evidence board.
- Unknown values remain N/A.

## Score to Beat

Every opportunity must answer:

1. Is there clear evidence people are buying this?
2. Can THE GRID produce an original, high-quality version?
3. Can we launch it within current resources?
4. Does it move Operation First Revenue forward?

If the answer is not supported by evidence, the opportunity remains in research.

## Doctrine

Launch Corps packages approved offers.
