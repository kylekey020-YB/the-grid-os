# Scout Reports Status

Current Version: v2.4.0

## Status

Manual/public-evidence only

## Mission

Scout Reports give Revenue Corps structured operational teeth for opportunity hunting without adding scraping, automation, marketplace account actions, or fake market data.

Scouts discover demand. Product Corps creates original assets. Launch Corps publishes only after Mission Commander approval.

## Reporting Line

- Scouts report to Revenue Architect.
- Revenue Architect ranks opportunities.
- Mission Commander approves experiments.
- Mission Pipeline decides what advances.

## Scout Teams

1. Etsy Scout
   - Digital product opportunities, tags, niches, buyer language, and pricing patterns.

2. Fiverr Scout
   - Productized service opportunities, pricing, gig positioning, and demand signals.

3. Gumroad Scout
   - Digital toolkit, template, and eBook opportunities.

4. Asset Scout
   - 2D game assets, UI kits, icon packs, sound FX, music loops, Unity, Godot, and Unreal opportunities.

5. Bounty Scout
   - GitHub bounties, hackathons, Kaggle competitions, AI contests, freelance RFPs, and grant opportunities.

6. Trend Scout
   - Seasonal, emerging, and fast-moving niches.

7. Review Scout
   - Buyer complaints, unmet needs, and "I wish this had" language.

8. Pricing Scout
   - Price ranges, bundle economics, premium positioning, and low-end saturation.

## Report Fields

Each report includes:

- scout name
- lane
- current status
- evidence source placeholder
- opportunity findings
- buyer pain
- competition notes
- margin potential
- speed to launch
- risk flags
- recommendation
- confidence
- next action

## Current Evidence State

Unknown values remain N/A until manual public evidence is recorded.

No report currently claims marketplace demand, revenue, margin, conversion, or opportunity confidence without evidence.

## Prohibited

- No scraping integrations.
- No platform-rule scraping.
- No account automation.
- No autonomous publishing.
- No customer messaging.
- No fake marketplace data.
- No fake reviews.
- No copied listings, designs, products, trademarks, or copyrighted characters.
- No fabricated revenue, demand, margin, conversion, or confidence.
