# Alpha Generation Doctrine

Version: Alpha Lab v1.0
Status: Research doctrine

## Mission

THE GRID does not hunt for one permanent trading strategy.

THE GRID builds a permanent research engine for discovering, testing, rejecting, improving, and compounding alpha hypotheses.

## Core Doctrine

- No strategy is permanent.
- Every hypothesis competes against evidence.
- Every model must continually earn its place.
- The edge is not one signal.
- The edge is the research engine.

## Continuous Improvement Doctrine

THE GRID does not seek a single perfect strategy.
THE GRID builds a machine for discovering, testing, rejecting, improving, and compounding alpha hypotheses.
The research engine is the edge.

## Evidence Doctrine

Alpha records are hypotheses until evidence proves otherwise.

A hypothesis may advance only when it has:

- real data provenance
- mechanical rules or a documented model contract
- no-lookahead validation
- realistic costs and slippage where applicable
- out-of-sample testing
- comparison against simple baselines
- drawdown and regime review
- Mission Commander approval before any promotion

## WRAITH-LSTM Doctrine

ALPHA-501 Sequential Liquidation Model is a signal-generation research hypothesis.

It does not assume that liquidations are momentum or contrarian. It tests both possibilities and lets evidence decide.

No alpha claim exists unless real historical liquidation data passes validation and the model beats simple baselines out-of-sample.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim:

- fake alpha
- fake backtests
- fake win rate
- fake expectancy
- fake PnL
- fake data quality
- live trading readiness
- broker readiness
- exchange readiness
- paper-trading readiness without approval

## Safety Boundary

Alpha Lab does not trade.

Alpha Lab does not connect to brokers, exchanges, wallets, or live accounts.

Alpha Lab records hypotheses and evidence gates only.
