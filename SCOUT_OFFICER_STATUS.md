# Scout Officer Layer

Current Version: v2.1.0

## Purpose

Create the first Scout Officer layer for opportunity discovery while keeping the organization clean and evidence-first.

## Reporting Line

Scouts report to Revenue Architect.

Risk Scout also reports risk signals to Sentinel.

## Scout Officers

### Market Scout

Role: discovers marketplace opportunities.

Status: Planned / Research Only.

### Demand Scout

Role: identifies buyer pain, search intent, and customer language.

Status: Planned / Research Only.

### Risk Scout

Role: flags saturation, copyright/trademark risk, ToS risk, and weak margin.

Status: Planned / Advisory Only.

## Rules

- Scouts do not publish.
- Scouts do not message customers.
- Scouts do not spend money.
- Scouts do not scrape against platform rules.
- Scouts only gather public evidence and create reports.
- Scouts do not copy listings, products, reviews, designs, or claims.
