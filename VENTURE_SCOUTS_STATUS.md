## Opportunity Scorecard

Every scout must answer the same questions before an opportunity enters the Mission Pipeline:

- Is there evidence people are paying for this?
- How fast could we launch?
- Can THE GRID create something original?
- Does it fit THE GRID's current capabilities?
- Is recurring revenue possible?
- What is the confidence score from 0 to 100?

Scores remain N/A until public/manual evidence exists.

## Rules

- Scouts discover business models, not just products.
- Scouts gather public/manual evidence and create reports.
- Scouts do not publish.
- Scouts do not message customers.
- Scouts do not spend money.
- Scouts do not automate accounts.
- Scouts do not scrape against platform rules.
- Scouts do not copy listings, products, channels, code, designs, or protected work.
- Revenue Architect ranks opportunities.
- Mission Commander approves experiments.

## Doctrine

Scouts discover demand.
Product Corps creates original assets.
Launch Corps publishes only after Mission Commander approval.
Venture Scouts discover lawful business models using public evidence.
Every opportunity uses the same scorecard before Mission Pipeline entry.
